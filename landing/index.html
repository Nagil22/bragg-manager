How it works

Three steps to a clean drive

01
Pick a folder to scan
Point Bragg Manager at any folder — your home directory, Downloads, Desktop, wherever. It walks every sub-folder and catalogues every file in seconds, even on drives with hundreds of thousands of files.
02
Review recommendations
The app surfaces grouped recommendations — duplicate sets, large files, old files, junk. Each card shows you exactly which files are affected, how much space you'll reclaim, and why the item was flagged. Enable AI for plain-English reasoning.
03
Delete or move — your choice
Approve a recommendation to delete or move files. Bragg Manager confirms before anything is touched, and shows you exactly what changed. Files removed from your drive are gone — no recovery bin required.

FAQ

Common questions

Will it delete my files without asking?
No. Bragg Manager always shows you exactly what it will remove before anything is touched. You approve every action. Deleting a batch of more than 5 files triggers an extra confirmation.
What does the AI enhancement actually do?
It sends your scanned file metadata (not the file contents) to Claude Haiku via OpenRouter. Claude refines the recommendations into plain English, explains why each item was flagged, and suggests where to move files. You need to add your own free OpenRouter API key in Settings.
What happens after my 4 free scans?
Your scan results are saved — you can still browse, search, and view all recommendations from your last scan even during the cooldown or after the trial ends. Only starting a new scan requires Pro after the 4 free scans.
Can Nigerians pay with a local card?
Yes — Gumroad accepts Visa and Mastercard including dollar-enabled Nigerian cards (GTBank, Zenith, UBA). If your Naira card is blocked for international transactions, a virtual dollar card (Grey, Chipper, Geegpay) works too.
Is this a subscription?
No. You pay once and own it. No renewal, no monthly fee. The license key works on both Mac and Windows.
How do I activate my license after purchase?
After payment on Gumroad you'll receive an email with a license key. Open Bragg Manager → Settings → paste the key → click Activate. The app unlocks immediately, no internet connection required after activation.
